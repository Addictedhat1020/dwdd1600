Target Shooting Blasters

In the target shooting blasters page you will find blasters that are good for shooting targets with.

Best Target Shooters

Basically all blasters are good for target shooting but springer blasters are usually better at target shooting than flywheel blasters

Target Shooting Blasters

Here are some of the best lines for target shooting blasters

  • Nerf Rival
  • Nerf Rival Edge Series

High powered blasters are also really good for target shooting

Purchase recommendations

If you want a good target shooting blaster than you probably want to get a higher powered blaster. The Rival Series is really good for target shooting and basically any high powered blaster is fun to shoot targets with. Keep in mind that these are only recommendations and you can buy whatever you want.

*warning* some of these blasters are old and outdated and may be DISCONTINUED and unavailable for purchase

The Saturn

The Nerf Rival Edge Series Saturn is a very good blaster for target shooting and is really fun to use.

The Jupiter

The Nerf Rival Edge Jupiter is a good target shooting blaster that looks like a sniper.
