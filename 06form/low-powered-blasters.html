In the low powered blasters section you will find low powered blasters

History Of Low Powered Blasters

Low powered blasters first started when the Nerf Sharpshooter was released in 1992. It was the first blaster made that shot foam darts. After the Sharpshooter was released Nerf N-Strike eventualy came along and then Nerf N-Strike Elite and all the stuff after that.

Low Powered Blasters

Here are some of the main places low powered blasters come from.

  • Nerf N-Strike Elite
  • Nerf N-Strike Elite 2.0
  • Walmart Adventure Force*

*Walmart Advenutre Force blasters are made by multiple dart blaster companies including Dart Zone, Buzzbee, and Zuru.

There are tons of low powered blasters that have been made so I will go over some of the best ones.

Nerf Jolt
A good small one shot blaster. The Jolt is a good backup blaster to have in your pocket.
Nerf Elite Rapidstrike
A fast shooting flywheel blaster. The Rapidstrike is a good flywheel blaster to use as a primary.
Nerf Elite Retaliator
A pump action springer blaster. The Retaliator is a very good pump action springer to use as a primary.
Nerf Stryfe
A smaller flywheel blaster. The Stryfe is a very good flywheel blaster that is tactical and can be used in many situations.
Nerf Elite Rampage
A pump action springer blaster. The Rampage is a good pump action springer and comes with a cool 25 round drum mag.
Nerf Elite Roughcut
A pump action springer blaster. The Roughcut is a good secondary shotgun blaster that can shoot 2 darts at a time.
Nerf Elite 2.0 Motoblitz
A flywheel blaster. The Motoblitz is a bullpup blaster with a 8 shot master key on the bottom.

Purchase recommendations

When purchasing a blaster you should choose carefully. I recommend staying away from the Elite 2.0 line because it has lots of janky, low-quality, crappy blasters that are not good. I would recommend staying away from Nerf's newer blasters because Nerf's newer products are just crappy reskins of older blasters and you can find much better quality blasters elsewhere. I would recommend getting something from the Elite line if you want a good blaster. These are just recommendations so you don't have to follow them.

Here are the blasters I recommend getting if you want a low powered blaster.

The Nerf Rapidstrike

The Rapidstrike is a good flywheel primary blaster and will be able to take down your targets fast.

The Nerf Retaliator

The Retaliator is a good springer primary and is a great basic blaster to bring to a Nerf war.

The Nerf Roughcut

The Roughcut is a great secondary blaster to use as a shotgun when you need it.

The Nerf Motoblitz

The Motoblitz is one of the only good blasters in the Elite 2.0 line and is a great primary. You can use the 8-shot master key when you are out of darts and in trouble and your enemies won't be expect it.

The Nerf Jolt

The Jolt is a great backup blaster to have in your pocket when you get in a sticky situation.
